What happens next

You will receive an email with a link for you to sign in to manage your case, view and update your question to Ofqual.

We take all questions seriously, and if you ask us a question you will be notified by email once we have an answer for you. You will need to follow the link within the email and sign in to read our response and manage your case.

We will reply within 20 working days, from the date that we have enough information to respond to your request. If we need clarification regarding your request, we will contact you as early as possible.

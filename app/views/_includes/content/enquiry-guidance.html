How will my enquiry be answered?

A member of our public enquiries team will read your enquiry and look to ensure that it is within the teamâ€™s remit to provide you with an answer.

When your enquiry is within our remit, we will write to you with the answer.

If an alternative team within Ofqual is best suited to respond to your enquiry directly, we will inform you that we have passed your enquiry to the relevant team.

Where your enquiry is within the remit of an alternative organisation, where we can, we will respond to you with the details of the organisation.

When will my enquiry be answered?

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 3 working days. This includes if we need to inform you that we have passed your enquiry to an alternative team within Ofqual.

Some enquiries can be more complex and take longer for us to gather enough information to provide a full response. Where our team need longer, we will keep you informed and update you with the progress of your enquiry at least every 5 working days.

Common enquiries

We get some queries quite frequently, such as:

  • How can I get a replacement certificate?
  • Who do you regulate?
  • Is my qualification regulated by Ofqual?
  • How does my qualification compare to others?

We’ve made some short videos and animations to answer these: https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/ask-ofqual-common-questions-answered

Enquiries we cannot answer

Certificates

Ofqual does not issue certificates, this is done by the exam board.

Qualification equivalences

Ofqual does not provide qualification equivalences as each regulated qualification has a level. If qualification types share the same level then they are seen as broadly comparable, in terms of the demand they place on the learner. However, qualifications at the same level can still be very different in terms of content and duration.

View a guide to understanding comparisons between qualification levels: https://www.gov.uk/what-different-qualification-levels-mean/compare-different-qualification-levels
